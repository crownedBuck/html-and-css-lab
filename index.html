Are you a beginner who's worried that your players will be board? Are you a DM where your players look more at their phone than on the battlefield? Well you've come to the right place!

There are many things a DM can do to get their players vested into the story. Some of which are:

  • Creating dynamic characters with interesting backstories that make the world feel lived in
  • Using props when applicable to give players an idea of what something they've recieved looks like.
  • Using lights color changing lights and candles to better get the players subconciously thinking about the location

The more players engage with the game, the less they will be playing on their phones when something 'important' happens, miss it, and need to ask you later. We ALL know that problem player.

Other ways 'outside' of game to get your placers invested is to have everyone bring a snack or even to have one person bring the snacks but switch the responsibility each week.
